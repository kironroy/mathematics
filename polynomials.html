Taxi

Suppose a driver wants to know how many miles he has to drive to earn $100.

If the meter charges the customer a rate of $1.50 a mile and the driver gets half of that

How many miles does he have to drive to get $100 dollars

this can be written in polynomial form as: 1/2 ($1.50)x = $100
